
# Areas of application

Like many other models, Gradient Boosting is widely applied to the usual problems of Machine Learning: Churn prediction, fraudulent transactions, disease diagnosis, asset price prediction,
etc. However, Gradient Boosting has been particularly useful in the domain of ranking, high energy physics and geoscience.

Commercial web search engines such as Yahoo and Yandex use variants for their ranking engines, so the website can better rank the results of a search based on specific parameters, like clicking history, word frequency, geographic location, dwell time and many others.

At the Large Hadron Collider (LHC), variants of Gradient Boosting successfully reproduced the outcome of non Machine Learning models on datasets that played a key role in identifying the Higgs boson.

In Geoscience, Boosting provided excellent results in predicting metals/minerals in specific regions, pollutants concentration in water, modelling earthquake susceptibility areas and classifying seismic signals.
